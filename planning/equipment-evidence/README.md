# /planning/equipment-evidence/ — pre-Phase 3 evidence holding area

Manufacturer specifications, datasheets, calibration certificates,
and other instrument documentation collected during equipment-
inventory work. Held here pending Phase 3, when the documents will
be promoted into `/qms/records/calibration/` (or other appropriate
sub-paths of `/qms/records/`) as controlled records, with proper
frontmatter, identification per clause 7.5.2, and entries in
`/qms/MASTER-DOCUMENT-LIST.md`.

This directory is part of `/planning/` and therefore **out of scope**
of the certification. Documents here are not yet controlled.

## Naming

`<slug>_<YYYY-MM-DD>.<ext>` — the date is the date the document was
retrieved or received. Source URL or other provenance is recorded
in `planning/equipment-inventory.md` alongside the entry for the
instrument the document covers.
