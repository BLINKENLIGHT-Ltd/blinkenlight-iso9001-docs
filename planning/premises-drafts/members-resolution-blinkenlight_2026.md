## Background

The directors wish to consider the company entering into the Co-occupation and
Shared-Facilities Agreement between the company and Pemberton Digital Ltd (the
"Agreement").

Both directors are interested in the Agreement:

- Patrick Coleman is also a director and a significant shareholder of Pemberton
  Digital Ltd; and
- Vytautas Rudys is also a minority shareholder of Pemberton Digital Ltd.

The company has adopted the model articles for private companies limited by
shares. Under article 14 of those articles, an interested director is not,
unless the members otherwise resolve under article 14(3)(a), to be counted in
the quorum or to vote on a matter in which he is interested. As both directors
are interested, this resolution is required so that the board can validly
consider and approve the Agreement.

## Resolution

IT IS RESOLVED, as an ordinary resolution, THAT:

pursuant to article 14(3)(a) of the company's articles of association, the
restriction in article 14 — which would otherwise prevent each of Patrick
Coleman and Vytautas Rudys, as directors interested in the Agreement, from
being counted, for quorum and voting purposes, in the directors'
decision-making in respect of the Agreement — be and is hereby disapplied in
respect of the Agreement.
